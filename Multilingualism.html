Multilingualism

Definition

Multilingualism can be defined as the ability of speaking two or more languages

Types

There are three types of Multilingualism which are:

  1. Bilingualism.
  2. Trilingualism.
  3. Polyglotism.

1. Bilingualism

This is the ability of speaking two languages.

Examples

  • A native English speaker being able to speak French.

  • A native French speaker having the ability to speak Spanish.

2.Trilingualism

This like the name implies is the ability of speaking three language.

3.Polyglot

A polyglot can be defined as a person who is able to commununicate in several languages.
